RESTORING ECOSYSTEMS EMPOWERING PEOPLE

A healthy environment and thriving communities are interconnected. We work to restore coral reefs, empower island communities, and invest in the next generation of environmental stewards.

OUR PROJECTS

Project Ah'kan is a Maldivian NGO dedicated to creating a sustainable future for the Maldives, where our vibrant culture and pristine environment thrive for generations to come. We work towards this by restoring coral reefs through our Coral Nursery project, nurturing and transplanting coral fragments to revive vital ecosystems and safeguard our coastlines. Furthermore, the Island 2050 project empowers island communities to adapt to climate change by promoting sustainable practices like renewable energy, waste management, and climate-resilient agriculture. Finally, the Next Gen project invests in the next generation of leaders by empowering Maldivian youth through education, leadership development, and environmental awareness programs, ensuring a sustainable and prosperous future for the Maldives.
